Play School and Pre-Kindergarten

Play school and Pre-Kindergarten of Sree Vinayasree Vidyanikethan is literally 'a garden for children' which gives every child a safe, open space to make the transition from home to school. As we all know that play is the highest form of research, the play-way kind of set-up allows the little ones to explore and to experience the full wonder of childhood at an unhurried pace.

It helps in building a positive association with learning in the form of fun activities and guided play. With individual attention a child is oriented to become aware of himself/herself and develop motor and cognitive skills.

Kindergarten Programme Overview

Kindergarten is the time when children begin to understand the dynamics of social interaction and their own individuality in the light of their mingling with others. Therefore, the Sree Vinayasree Vidyanikethan Kindergarten Program Statement has been suitably designed which describes clear expectations in seven learning areas.

🔤 Early Literacy

Introduction to letters, sounds, and basic reading skills through interactive and engaging activities that make learning fun and memorable.

🔢 Early Numeracy

Foundation mathematical concepts including numbers, counting, patterns, and basic problem-solving skills through hands-on learning.

🏛️ Citizenship

Understanding community roles, respect for others, and basic civic responsibilities appropriate for young learners.

👤 Identity and Individuality

Self-awareness, personal identity development, and understanding of unique qualities and characteristics of each child.

🌍 Environment and Community Awareness

Understanding the world around them, nature appreciation, and basic environmental consciousness.

🤝 Personal and Social Responsibility

Developing empathy, sharing, cooperation, and understanding of personal responsibilities in a social setting.

🏃‍♂️ Physical Skills

Gross and fine motor skill development through physical activities, games, and coordinated movements.

🎨 Creative Expression

Encouraging creativity through art, music, drama, and various forms of self-expression and imagination.

Age Groups and Programs

🍼 Playgroup (2-3 years)

Introduction to group activities, basic social skills, and gentle separation from parents in a caring environment.

🧸 Pre-KG (3-4 years)

Foundation skills development, pre-literacy activities, and preparation for formal kindergarten education.

📖 Lower KG (4-5 years)

Introduction to formal learning concepts while maintaining a play-based approach to education.

🎓 Upper KG (5-6 years)

School readiness preparation, advanced pre-literacy and numeracy skills for primary school transition.

Daily Schedule

  • 9:00 - 9:30 AM: Welcome and Free Play
  • 9:30 - 10:15 AM: Circle Time and Learning Activities
  • 10:15 - 10:45 AM: Snack Time and Social Interaction
  • 10:45 - 11:30 AM: Outdoor Play and Physical Activities
  • 11:30 - 12:15 PM: Creative Arts and Crafts
  • 12:15 - 1:00 PM: Story Time and Quiet Activities
  • 1:00 - 2:00 PM: Lunch and Rest Time
  • 2:00 - 2:30 PM: Preparation for Home Time
